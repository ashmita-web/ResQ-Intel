# ResQ-Intel: Disaster Intelligence Mapping Platform (DIMP)

**ResQ-Intel (DIMP)** is a real-time, AI-powered disaster response platform designed to provide actionable insights to emergency management teams. It fuses satellite imagery, drone footage, and social media signals to detect damaged infrastructure, flood-affected zones, and population displacement.

## Overview

Disasters require quick and accurate situational awareness to save lives. DIMP enables emergency responders to focus on the most affected areas **within minutes instead of hours** by providing:

- **Multi-modal data fusion:** Combines satellite, drone, and social media inputs.  
- **Comprehensive situational awareness:** Generates live, layered disaster maps.  
- **Scalability & Open Source:** Easily adapted by governments, NGOs, and research institutes.  
- **Future-proof design:** Supports predictive flood modeling and voice-based SOS localization.

---

## Features

- **Real-time Disaster Detection:** Identify damaged infrastructure, floods, and displaced populations.  
- **Live Layered Maps:** Visualize affected areas dynamically for rapid decision-making.  
- **Alerts & Integration:** Push notifications for new critical incidents; REST API for integration with national emergency services and NGOs.  
- **Search & Filter:** Query by region, type of damage, and urgency.  
- **Map Export & Reports:** Generate PDFs, GeoTIFFs, or KMZ for sharing with teams.

---

## Dashboard

The DIMP dashboard provides:

- Real-time updates for emergency responders.  
- Filters to prioritize high-urgency areas.  
- Exportable disaster maps for reporting and coordination.
